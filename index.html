Your Questions, Answered

Navigate Amsterdam's underground scene like a local—here's everything you need to know about joining our community

What exactly is "If It Ain't Dutch It Ain't Much"?

We're your insider guide to Amsterdam's underground electronic music scene. Think of us as your local crew—we share secret venue intel, exclusive afterparty locations, and real-time updates during ADE 2026. No tourist traps, no mainstream fluff, just authentic techno culture.

How do I join the community?

Hit the access request button below and tell us why you're into underground electronic music. We're looking for genuine heads, not clout chasers. Most applications get reviewed within 24 hours—if you're real, you're in.

What do members actually get?

Full access to our ADE 2026 survival guides, weekly event calendars with hidden gems, artist spotlights, budget accommodation tips, and a community of 5000+ like-minded ravers. Plus, you'll get venue coordinates 24 hours before events drop publicly.

Is there a membership fee?

Nope, it's 100% free. We're not here to make money off the culture—we're here to preserve it. This is a community-run project by ravers, for ravers. No paywalls, no premium tiers, just pure underground love.

Why should I trust your ADE recommendations?

Our team lives in Amsterdam and has been deep in the scene for years. We skip the Instagram-famous spots and focus on where locals actually go—the warehouse raves in Noord, afterhours in hidden basements, and intimate club nights that sell out in minutes. Real recognizes real.

Can I browse without creating an account?

Some content is public—think blog posts and general guides. But the real gold (secret locations, invite-only events, insider tips) is members-only. We keep it exclusive to protect the venues and maintain the underground vibe.

When is ADE 2026, and when should I start planning?

ADE 2026 runs October 21-25, the 30 Years ADE anniversary edition. Start planning NOW—accommodations sell out fast, and the best secret events get announced weeks in advance. We're already dropping venue guides and artist spotlights to help you prep.

Is this just for ADE, or year-round?

Year-round, baby! ADE is our Super Bowl, but Amsterdam's underground scene never stops. We cover weekly warehouse parties, Shelter's Sunday marathons, Paradiso's legendary nights, and pop-up raves that materialize out of nowhere.

I'm coming from abroad—will you help me navigate?

Absolutely! Our guides cover everything: budget hostels near the action, how public transport works at 4am, which coffee shops to avoid, where to find proper stroopwafels, and—most importantly—how to respect Amsterdam's club culture. We got you.

How quickly do you update event info?

Real-time during ADE week. Venue changes, last-minute additions, cancellations—if it happens, you'll know within the hour. Outside ADE season, we update weekly with upcoming events worth your time.

Can I contribute content or share my own discoveries?

We love that energy! Once you're a verified member, you can submit venue reviews, party reports, and artist recommendations. The best contributions get featured in our weekly digest. This is a community—help us keep it thriving.

What's your vibe check policy?

Zero tolerance for bad behavior. Respect the venues, respect the locals, respect the music. Share secret locations responsibly. Don't be that person who ruins it for everyone. If you can't hang, we'll revoke access—no warnings, no drama.
